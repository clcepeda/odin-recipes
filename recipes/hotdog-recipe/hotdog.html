Hotdog Recipe

Hot dogs are American as hell. Eat one and be a patriot!

    Ingredients
  • Hotdog
  • Bun
  • Condiments
    Steps
  1. Grill the Hotdog until slightly charded on all sides.
  2. Lightly toast the bun.
  3. Place the Hotdog in the bun.
  4. Dress the top of the Hotdog with desired condiments.
  5. Enjoy!
